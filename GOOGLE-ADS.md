## Key Decisions

- **Display Network OFF** — Search only. Display wastes budget on passive impressions.
- **Search Partners ON** — Includes Google Maps, high value for "near me" queries.
- **Presence only targeting** — Prevents wasted spend on people researching South Florida from elsewhere.
- **Exact + phrase match only** — All keywords switched from broad match to exact/phrase on April 9. Broad match was burning budget on irrelevant queries (yard cleanup, competitor names, self-dump searches).
- **Maximize Conversions bidding** — Requires GA4 conversion tracking to be linked and firing correctly to work well.
- **Ad Groups 3 & 4 (Furniture/Appliance, Cleanouts) deferred to Month 2** — Budget too thin to learn from 4 groups simultaneously. Revisit after first 2 weeks of data.
- **Photos made optional on estimate form** — Originally required, changed to optional to reduce friction.
